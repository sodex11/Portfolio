About Me

I’m Kazeem Sodik Isola — an online Mathematics, Physics, and Electronic/Electrical Engineering tutor, and a hands-on engineer/software developer. I teach with structure and results in mind. No fluff. Concepts first, problems second, mastery last.

I’ve taught learners preparing for engineering degrees and supported undergraduates on core modules. I balance a traditional respect for fundamentals with a practical, forward-looking approach: explain clearly, solve rigorously, verify outcomes.

Education: B.Eng. Electronic & Electrical Engineering (Second Class Upper), Obafemi Awolowo University.

Teaching Philosophy

  • Start from first principles; avoid shortcuts until concepts are secure.
  • Use worked examples and variations to build depth and speed.
  • Measure progress with targeted questions and honest feedback.
